The National Level Technical Fest organized by the Computer Science Department and Cybernauts Club promises to be an exhilarating event at Yenepoya Institute of Technology. Led by coordinators Munavvar Sinan K P, Nishamil P, and Vishal Ravi, along with faculty coordinator Prof. Sleeba Mathew, this fest is set to ignite innovation and collaboration.

With over 11 competitions boasting a prize pool exceeding 500k, participants can dive into coding, robotics, embedded systems, startups, and more. The event spans two dynamic days, offering attendees the chance to engage in insightful talks and workshops, including a thrilling 24-hour hackathon. Renowned software developer Ishan Sharma, known for his work with HackerRank, will headline the fest, inspiring participants to push the boundaries of technological advancement.

The fest provides a unique platform for students and tech enthusiasts to showcase their talents, exchange ideas, and forge valuable connections with industry experts. Beyond the competitive spirit, participants can immerse themselves in hands-on workshops, interactive demonstrations, and panel discussions on emerging trends in technology. With a focus on fostering creativity and problem-solving skills, the event aims to empower the next generation of innovators to tackle real-world challenges and drive positive change in society. Whether delving into the intricacies of coding algorithms or exploring the latest advancements in robotics, attendees are sure to find inspiration and opportunities for growth at this vibrant gathering of tech enthusiasts.
